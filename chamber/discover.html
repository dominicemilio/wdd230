About Guatapé

Guatapé, a colorful town in Colombia, is famous for its vibrant zócalos, stunning landscapes, and El Peñol rock.

  • Population: 7,000+
  • Climate: Mild & Tropical
  • Economy: Tourism, Agriculture, Handicrafts

Culture & Attractions

Explore Guatapé’s rich history, art, and adventure activities, from boat tours to climbing El Peñol.

Business & Growth

Guatapé offers unique opportunities for entrepreneurs in tourism, hospitality, and local crafts.
