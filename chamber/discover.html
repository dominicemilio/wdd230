About Guatape

Guatapé, a colorful town in Colombia, is famous for its vibrant zócalos, stunning landscapes, and El Peñol rock.

  • Population: 7,000+
  • Climate: Mild & Tropical
  • Economy: Tourism, Agriculture, Handicrafts
