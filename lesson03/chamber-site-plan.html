Site Name

The Guatape Chamber of Commerce. This name represents Guatape, a beautiful town located in the Andes mountains and known for its gorgeous lake and colorful architecture. The name reflects the town's appeal and its focus on promoting local businesses and tourism.

Site Purpose

  • To take this growing tourist attraction and its businesses to the next level.
  • To exhibit Guatape's unique history, culture, and natural beauty, attracting both tourists and investors. This website's mission is to promote tourism, and to leave both the business owner and the tourist with an enjoyable and productive experience.
  • To support local businesses by providing networking opportunities, resources, and a platform to promote their products and services.

Scenarios

1) Lena Schmidt - A Backpacker from Germany

Persona: Lena is a 24-year-old German woman who is backpacking through Colombia. She loves adventure, although she has to be budget-minded in everything she does. She absolutely loves nature. She is fluent in English and speaks Spanish at an intermediate level.

Scenario: Lena walks into the Chamber of Commerce, wearing a heavy backpack. She walks toward the reception desk with a friendly smile and asks in Spanish, "Buenos dias. I'm Lena from Germany, and I'm travelling all throughout South America, but specifically here in Colombia. I've heard wonderful things about Guatape, and I'd like to know if there are any good hiking trails, and places to camp. Do you have any recommendations?"

Her Chamber of Commerce Needs: Hiking trail maps, with difficulty levels and approximate walking time. It is also necessary to have information on permitted camping areas, along with information on local guides. It would be good to provide tour information.

2) George and Martha Miller - Retirees from Florida

Persona: George and Martha are married retirees in their late 60s from Florida. They love travelling and exploring new cultures and cuisine. They also appreciate hand-crafted items and look for unique ones wherever they visit. They really like to travel comfortably and enjoy guided tours.

Scenario: Martha says "Hello, we're George and Martha Miller from Florida. We are visiting Guatape and are impressed by the colorful houses. We are interested in local handicrafts and like to know where we can find them. We would also like to know where we can order authentic Colombian cuisine, like the 'Bandeja Paisa'. Could you recommend these to us?"

Their Chamber of Commerce Needs: Information on local artisans and their workshops. Recommendations for restaurants that serve traditional Colombian food. Information on guided tours, with area maps.

3) Mateo Vargas – Small Business Owner in Guatape

Persona: Mateo is a 35-year-old resident of Guatape who owns a small restaurant that specializes in traditional Paisa cuisine. He works hard and is passionate about his restaurant, but he doesn’t attract enough tourists to keep his business open.

Scenario: Mateo enters the Chamber of Commerce with a rather worried look on his face. He says hello to the chamber representative and explains his situation. He tells her that he owns a small restaurant just off the main square. He then explains the type of cuisine that they prepare with authentic food from recipes that have been passed down through generations. He then continues by telling the chamber representative how difficult things have been for lack of tourists. She explains to him that there may be some local advertising that he could participate in.

His Chamber of Commerce Needs: Local tourism information and marketing opportunities. He could also use some connections at hotels and tour operators who could help promote his business. He could also be included in the local tourism brochure.

Color Schema

  • Yellow (#FFCD00): Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and important icons.
  • Blue (#0033A0): Used for headers, navigation menus, and accents.
  • Red (#CE1126): Used sparingly for alerts, links, and accent borders.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for backgrounds and main text areas.
  • Light Gray (#F4F4F4): Used for secondary backgrounds and sections.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Poppins", sans-serif - Used for all headings and navigation menus to convey a modern, clean look.
  • Body Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for paragraphs and general content to ensure readability.
  • Special Accent Font: "Playfair Display", serif - Used sparingly for quotes and special sections to add elegance.

Wireframe

Desktop View: A horizontal navigation bar at the top, a wide hero section, a multi-column layout for featured content, and footer links at the bottom.

Mobile View: A vertical layout with a collapsible navigation menu, a hero section for promotions, followed by featured content and misc. content stacked vertically.
