Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

The Guatapé Chamber of Commerce. This name represents Guatapé, a picturesque town nestled in the Andes mountains and known for its stunning lake and colorful architecture. The name reflects the town's charm and its focus on promoting local businesses and tourism.

Site Purpose

  • To showcase Guatapé's unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, attracting tourists and investors alike.
  • To support local businesses by providing networking opportunities, resources, and a platform to promote their services and products.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-see attractions and activities in Guatapé?
  • Where can I find a directory of local shops, restaurants, and tour operators?
  • How can I learn about upcoming cultural events and festivals in the town?

Color Schema

  • Yellow (#FFCD00): Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and important icons.
  • Blue (#0033A0): Used for headers, navigation menus, and accents.
  • Red (#CE1126): Used sparingly for alerts, links, and accent borders.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for backgrounds and main text areas.
  • Light Gray (#F4F4F4): Used for secondary backgrounds and sections.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Poppins", sans-serif - Used for all headings and navigation menus to convey a modern, clean look.
  • Body Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for paragraphs and general content to ensure readability.
  • Special Accent Font: "Playfair Display", serif - Used sparingly for quotes and special sections to add elegance.

Wireframe

Mobile View: A vertical layout with a collapsible navigation menu, a hero section for promotions, followed by featured content and call-to-actions stacked vertically.

Desktop View: A horizontal navigation bar at the top, a wide hero section, a three-column layout for featured content, and footer links at the bottom.
